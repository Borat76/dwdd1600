Swinging

Steps of a Baseball Swing

  1. Stance: Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, knees slightly bent, and your bat held at your shoulder.
  2. Load: Shift your weight back slightly, cocking your wrists, and bringing the bat back.
  3. Stride: Step forward with your front foot, transferring your weight to your front leg.
  4. Swing: Rotate your hips and shoulders, swinging the bat through the zone.
  5. Follow-through: Continue your swing until the bat is pointing toward the pitcher's mound.
  6. Finish: End your swing with your bat pointing up towards the sky.
